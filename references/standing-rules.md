## Autonomy Levels

| Level | Behavior |
|-------|----------|
| **Full autonomy** | Draft AND send. No approval, no preview. |
| **Draft-only** | Draft reply, show for user review. Never send without approval. |
| **Priority flag** | Flag for immediate attention. Never archive or ignore. |
